Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj was the second ruler of the Maratha Empire and the eldest son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Known for his bravery and military skill, he fiercely defended the Maratha Empire against the Mughal Empire under Aurangzeb. Despite his short reign, he is celebrated for his resistance to the Mughals and his sacrifice, for which he is revered as a "dharmaveer" .
